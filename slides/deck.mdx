Lottie is a library that parses Adobe After Effects animations exported as JSON. It's available for Android, iOS, Web, React Native, and Windows

---

## What you need

1.  The Bodymovin or LottieFiles Plugin for AfterEffects
2.  Adobe AfterEffects
